  • Deposition Rate Optimization for Semiconductor Materials

    Semiconductor R&D

    What?

    • ‚Ä¢ Optimization of the semiconductor deposition rate is critical for improving quality and reducing manufacturing costs.
    • ‚Ä¢ The deposition rate affects properties like film thickness, uniformity, and purity that impact device performance and yield.
    • ‚Ä¢ Faster deposition can increase production throughput and lower capital costs.
    • ‚Ä¢ There is a complex relationship between process parameters like temperature and deposition rate that is difficult to optimize manually.
    • ‚Ä¢ Bayesian optimization is an advanced machine learning technique to efficiently search high-dimensional parameter spaces.
    • ‚Ä¢ It constructs a probabilistic model to represent belief over the objective function.
    • ‚Ä¢ The model is used to determine informative sample locations to evaluate.
    • ‚Ä¢ It balances exploration to improve the model and exploitation to find the optimum.

    How?

    • ‚Ä¢ An empirical deposition rate model was defined as a function of temperature and plasma power.
    • ‚Ä¢ The model has a quadratic relationship between temperature and deposition rate.
    • ‚Ä¢ Deposition rate increases linearly with higher plasma power.
    • ‚Ä¢ The model allows evaluating deposition rate at different parameter settings.
    • ‚Ä¢ A Gaussian process probabilistic model was constructed to represent belief over the deposition rate.
    • ‚Ä¢ Gaussian processes can capture complex nonlinear functions.
    • ‚Ä¢ The GP model is iteratively updated to improve accuracy.
    • ‚Ä¢ The model guides the selection of optimal parameters to evaluate next.

    Results?

    • ‚Ä¢ The optimized deposition rate was 65.25 A«í/s, a 30% improvement over the baseline.
    • ‚Ä¢ The higher deposition rate can enable faster production and lower costs.
    • ‚Ä¢ Film quality will also benefit from the optimized parameters.
    • ‚Ä¢ Bayesian optimization efficiently searched the parameter space to determine the optimal configuration.
    • ‚Ä¢ Only nine evaluations of the deposition model were required.
    • ‚Ä¢ The algorithm quickly converged despite the small data requirement.
    • ‚Ä¢ Bayesian optimization successfully maximized the deposition rate in the model by 30%.
    • ‚Ä¢ Significant benefits in terms of throughput, quality, and cost reduction are expected.
    • ‚Ä¢ This methodology provides an automated, efficient way to optimize complex manufacturing processes.
    • ‚Ä¢ The optimization framework is flexible to other processes and objective functions.
    • ‚Ä¢ Bayesian optimization accelerates parameter optimization compared to conventional methods.

    • ‚Ä¢ Then we built the first chamber and placed the 1st HEPA/ MANN air Filter with a range of 10 microns.
    • ‚Ä¢ In the Second chamber we arranged 12 MS pipes with a diameter of 1 inch each.
    • ‚Ä¢ In the third chamber, we have arranged HEPA air Filter with a range of 2.5 microns on its outlet.
    • ‚Ä¢ After that we set the outlet portion on it then we provide a door fully sealed to prevent air leakages by using a high-temperature packing sheet.

    • ‚Ä¢ The number of singular values kept determines the balance between compression ratio and image quality.
    • ‚Ä¢ Both the original and compressed images are displayed for visual comparison and analysis.
